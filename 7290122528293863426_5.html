LinkedIn Draft Bug Workaround

LinkedIn has a long-standing bug in which it cannot display drafts of its own articles within the LinkedIn app. Please do the following:

  1. Click the share icon in the upper right corner (inside LinkedIn).
  2. Share to your preferred browser.
  3. Once the page reopens in that browser, check below. If you see a real browser name (not LinkedIn) listed, you can click the link below to go to the article:

Article link: https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/draft/preview/7290122528293863426/?companyAuthorUrn=urn:li:fsd_company:105344256
