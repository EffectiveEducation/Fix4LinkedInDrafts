LinkedIn Draft Bug Workaround

LinkedIn has a long-standing bug in which it cannot display drafts of its own articles within the LinkedIn app. To access the article, please follow these steps:

  1. Click the share icon in the upper right corner.
  2. Share it with the web browser of your choice.
  3. Once in that web browser, click this link: https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/draft/preview/7290122528293863426/?companyAuthorUrn=urn:li:fsd_company:105344256
