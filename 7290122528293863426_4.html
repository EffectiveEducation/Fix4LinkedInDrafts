LinkedIn Draft Bug Workaround

LinkedIn has a long-standing bug in which it cannot display drafts of its own articles within the LinkedIn app. Please do the following:

  1. Click the share icon in the upper right corner (inside LinkedIn).
  2. Share to your preferred browser.
  3. Once the page reopens in that browser, this page will automatically take you to the article. If it doesn't, click this link: https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/draft/preview/7290122528293863426/?companyAuthorUrn=urn:li:fsd_company:105344256
